# Thesis Hotel — Pilot Deployment

**Accelerate Robotics' first commercial robot deployment.** 10-story hotel property in Miami. Phase 1 kickoff meeting was 2026-03-23 with Head of Engineering and Facility Ops.

## Status at a glance

- **Site:** The Thesis Hotel, Miami — 10 stories, guest floors 4–10
- **Phase 1:** 30-day C30 cleaning robot pilot on carpeted paths
- **Phase 2:** Elevator integration (IO ports 5–14 on Keenon E-Box)
- **Future:** W3 for luggage/room service, T8 for lobby tray service, lobby hardwood cleaning
- **Stakeholders:** Head of Engineering, Facility Ops

## Why Thesis Hotel first

- **Friendly customer** — engaged Head of Engineering and Facility Ops willing to pilot
- **Right size** — 10 stories is big enough to prove elevator integration matters, small enough to iterate quickly
- **Multiple robot types** — carpet cleaning, hard-floor cleaning, delivery, tray service — all in one building
- **Reference deployment** — success here becomes the case study for the next 10 buildings
