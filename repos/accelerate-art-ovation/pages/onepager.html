What Guests Experience

Tzeva Room Service

Chef Lumpkin's Mediterranean small plates delivered by a coral-accented W3 with a rotating Ringling art panel. Guest opens the door, takes the tray, pushes the cart back to the hall when done.

Late-Night Tzeva & Perspective

Nightcap at 12:30 AM? A second W3 presents items at the guest-room door in a leather-lined compartment after the bar closes. No staff call-back. No quality drop after midnight.

Invisible Floor Care

Guest-floor patterned carpet, lobby tile, ballroom, and Perspective rooftop maintained 2 AM–6 AM by C40 and C30. Guests wake to gallery-grade floors with no cleaning cart in a corridor.

Guest-Yield Protocol

Robots always yield to waiting guests and hold for the next elevator car. Guests never wait for a robot — guest-yield is written into the fleet control logic and auditable from the dashboard.
