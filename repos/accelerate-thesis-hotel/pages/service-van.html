Miami Market Red Flags — Do Not Pursue

  • "Finance-bait" listings — Hollywood dealers post $10-14K headline prices on 2019+ vans; it's actually "$2K down" advertised payment, not sale price
  • 2023 Ford Transit 350 @ $19,999 (Miami, "Roberto") — implausibly cheap, almost certainly salvage/rebuilt title
  • 2008 Dodge Sprinter @ $23,500 — pre-emissions era, overpriced
  • 2016 Sprinter @ $12,900 (272K mi) — mileage disqualifies
Archived — Compact Alternative

Ram ProMaster City

If the plan ever needs to pivot to a smaller van — e.g. a garage clearance constraint — the Ram ProMaster City is the only worth-considering compact. 74.2" tall (fits under 6'7"), ~131 cu ft cargo, 48.4" wheel-well to wheel-well, ~1,880 lb payload. Closest thing to a mini full-size for shelving.

Example listing: 2017 ProMaster City, $6,999, Miramar (clean title, 1 owner).

Climate control

Miami heat is the enemy of robot batteries and your soldering hand. An uninsulated cargo van hits 120 °F in 30 minutes. Lithium degrades fast above 120 °F.

Mobile WiFi
Decision Cellular-first, dual-carrier. Netgear Nighthawk M6 Pro + T-Mobile Business 100GB + Verizon backup SIM. ~$700 hardware, $110–130/mo. Skip Starlink Mini as the primary — urban Miami garages and loading docks have no sky view, and 5G uploads are actually faster than Starlink in those spots.

07 Action Plan

Next 30 days.

From purchase to first deployment.

1

Week 1 — Verify & inspect

  • Call West Park dealer on ProMaster — confirm available, request VIN
  • Pull Carfax + NMVTIS (flood check — critical in South Florida)
  • Demand dash mileage photo before visiting
  • Book PPI at European Diesel Shop or Andy's Auto
  • Call Hollywood dealer on Sprinter backup candidate
2

Week 2 — Purchase

  • PPI results reviewed — pass or walk away
  • Test drive both top candidates
  • Negotiate — ProMasters depreciate fast, aim $20-21K out the door
  • Purchase + title transfer (commercial auto insurance activated)
  • Schedule wrap design kickoff (2-week lead time starts now)
3

Week 3 — Build-out

  • Order Ranger Design cargo package (shelving, partition, ladder rack)
  • Order Goal Zero Yeti 3000X + 200W solar panel
  • Order tools + spare parts starter kit (Packout, Fluke, LiDAR spares, batteries)
  • Install cargo package (DIY weekend or local installer)
  • Wrap design approved, production begins
4

Week 4 — Launch

  • Apply wrap + magnetic co-brand panels
  • Full load-out — tools, parts, robots, consumables
  • Stage overnight shake-down drive (test GPS, tie-downs)
  • Ready for Phase 1 kickoff
