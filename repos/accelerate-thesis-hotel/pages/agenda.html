Thesis Hotel — Robot Deployment

Tactical alignment on the 30-day Phase 1 robot deployment, technical infrastructure requirements, elevator integration roadmap, and multi-robot expansion vision.

15 min
Item 1

Introductions & Project Overview

  • Accelerate Robotics overview — "One Brain, Many Bots" platform
  • Why The Thesis Hotel as the anchor deployment

Item 2

Phase 1: 30-Day C-30 Deployment Plan

  • Week 1: Installation, LIDAR/SLAM mapping, WiFi validation
  • Week 2: Controlled single-floor operations with staff shadowing
  • Week 3: Expanded multi-floor operations, guest feedback
  • Week 4: Full autonomous operations, KPI review, go/no-go
  • Metrics: completion rate, uptime, staff intervention, guest satisfaction
15 min
Item 3

Carpeted Path Area & Robot Coverage

  • C-30 operating zone: guest room corridors, Levels 4–10
  • Floor plan walkthrough — carpet transitions, thresholds, restricted zones
  • Service area access: clean/soiled linen closets, ice machine rooms
  • Charging station placement and staging area logistics
15 min
Item 4

Technical Infrastructure Requirements

  • WiFi audit — signal strength (-65 dBm min), dead zone identification
  • Dedicated robot VLAN/SSID separate from guest WiFi
  • Power requirements for charging stations (110V/15A)
  • Staff training: front desk (4 hrs), housekeeping (2 hrs), engineering (8 hrs)
15 min
Item 5

Phase 2: Elevator Integration Roadmap

  • Current elevator system — manufacturer, model, controller type
  • Keenon E-Box retrofit — hardware, RFID floor detection, IO wiring
  • Service elevator vs. guest elevator strategy
  • 10-story coverage: Lobby through Floor 10 (11 of 32 IO channels)
  • Installation timeline and elevator maintenance coordination
15 min
Item 6

Future Vision: Multi-Robot Fleet

  • Luggage transport: Autonomous bellhop robots for guest luggage
  • Room service: Kitchen-to-room food and amenity delivery
  • Lobby: Hard floor cleaning robots for hardwood and tile
  • Fleet coordination — sharing elevators, corridors, and schedules
  • Phased rollout: one use case at a time, each building on the last
10 min
Item 7

Resources, Timeline & Next Steps

  • Staff champions: 1–2 per shift as robot operations leads
  • Key contacts and escalation paths
  • Hardware delivery and installation timeline
  • Weekly milestone check-ins during 30-day pilot
  • Success criteria for Phase 2 go/no-go decision
