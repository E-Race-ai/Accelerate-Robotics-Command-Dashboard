Almost there

Wait ~30 seconds. The dashboard opens automatically when the backend is up. (Already copied to your clipboard — just paste.)

Uninstall WiFi Vision Extreme

Stops the background service, removes the launchd auto-start entry, and deletes ~/Library/Application Support/WiFiVisionExtreme/.

curl -fsSL https://acceleraterobotics.ai/installers/wifi-vision-uninstall.sh | bash

Paste that into Terminal. The launcher will reset itself when uninstall finishes.
